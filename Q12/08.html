This interesting place has 15 different types of Dolmens belonging to Neolithic age and some of which belongs to the Iron age which make this place best for Munnar sightseeing. These prehistoric dolmens are situated around 55 km from Munnar and are worth taking a long trip to reach and witness the remnants of Dolmenoid cists which date back to the Megalithic age.
